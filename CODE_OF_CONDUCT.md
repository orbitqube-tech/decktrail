# Code of conduct

DeckTrail is a small project. This is short on purpose.

## The standard

Treat everyone here as you would a colleague you respect and will work with again. Assume good
faith, argue about the work rather than the person, and take correction as well as you give it.
Disagreement is welcome and is most of how the project improves; contempt is not.

That covers almost everything. Concretely, and not exhaustively:

- Harassment, insults, and personal attacks are not tolerated, in issues, merge requests, commit
  messages, or anywhere else carrying the project's name.
- Do not publish anyone's private information.
- Keep it on topic. This is a tool for hosting documents, not a venue for anything else.

## Scope

This applies in every space the project uses, and to anyone taking part: reporting a bug, sending
a change, or commenting on someone else's.

## If something goes wrong

Email **info@orbitqube.com**. Reports are read by the maintainer and kept confidential. You will
get a reply, and a clear account of what was decided.

The maintainer enforces this, and holds themselves to it first. A response is proportionate to
what happened, and ranges from a private word to removal from the project's spaces. The aim is a
project that is calm to work on, not a tribunal.
