# CLA signatures

Everyone who has signed the [DeckTrail Contributor Licence Agreement](CLA.md).

Add yourself here in your first merge request, and add the
`DeckTrail-CLA-1.0-signed-off-by:` trailer to your commits. See "How to sign" in `CLA.md`.

```
- Full Name <email@example.com>  signed DeckTrail CLA v1.0 on YYYY-MM-DD
```

The file is the record. It is in the repository rather than in a service somewhere so that it
is auditable by anyone, outlives whatever tooling we use, and does not depend on a forge
account or a bot.
